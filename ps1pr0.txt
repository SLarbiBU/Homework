When this article was written that was a respectively new concept. Since the article has been written, however, not only have we gained access to an enormous amount of data, but we have also learned new ways to understand and manipulate it. Everyday members of society, not just scholars familiar with computers, have accepted the concept of the “cloud” and essential data storing utility worldwide. Today, every person can link up their phones to their computers, watches, ipods, credit cards, and even fitness trackers. Data has become accessible to the general public.
